List of social media applications

  • Linkedin

    • - A professional networking platform where users can create resumes, connect with colleagues and industry professionals, and search for jobs. It's widely used for career development, business networking, and recruiting.

    • - A photo and video sharing app that allows users to post content to their feed, share stories that disappear after 24 hours, and interact through likes, comments, and direct messages. It's especially popular among influencers, brands, and creatives.

  • Facebook

    • - A global social networking platform where users can create profiles, connect with friends, share photos, videos, and status updates, and join groups and communities based on shared interests.
